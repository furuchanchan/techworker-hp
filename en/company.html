Message Message from the CEO

Kotaro Furuno

CEO / Representative Director, TechWorker Inc. @koutarou_furuno

Generative AI is advancing every single day. Yet very few companies are actually putting it to work in their operations.

The reason is simple. A company's operational knowledge, decision criteria, and unwritten rules—its "context"—are not organized in a form that AI can use.

We specialize in this "context engineering," and we have supported 37 companies and 2,500 professionals, including publicly listed enterprises.

We are not a company that sells AI tools. Our work is to create the state in which your business itself is fundamentally transformed by AI.
Director Director

Toshiyuki Yamamoto

Director, TechWorker Inc.
Founder of Chatwork / CEO of Power Angels / Representative Director of the Japan Angel Investors Association

I founded Chatwork, an IT tool, and set out to bring it to non-IT industries. In 2018 I handed the CEO role to my co-founder and younger brother, and the following year, in 2019, the company was listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange Mothers market (with a market cap of over ¥50 billion).

Through that experience, I became certain of one thing. No matter how excellent the technology is, it will never be used inside a company without someone to bridge it to the realities of frontline operations.

The same thing is now happening with generative AI, a new technology. TechWorker will serve as that bridge and drive AI adoption across Japanese enterprises.
