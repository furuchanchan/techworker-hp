2026.03.14 Announcement

We refreshed our service lineup

Effective March 14, 2026, TechWorker Inc. has fully refreshed its service lineup. We have reorganized our offering into three program tiers matched to your AI adoption phase, so the scope of support we provide is clearer than ever.

Why we made the change

Since our founding, we have provided enterprise generative AI consulting and training. What became clear through supporting 37 companies and 2,500 people is that the challenges enterprises face with AI vary dramatically depending on the adoption phase they're in.

A company that simply wants to improve the work in front of it needs fundamentally different support from one ready to build a company-wide, AI-driven operating model. We took those lessons and structured them into three distinct program tiers.

The new program structure

Program 01 | AI Quick Start: change the work first
A "private AI" connected to your internal data that improves multiple workflows within three months. Built for executives and department heads at small and mid-sized companies, it covers AI workflow design at the task level and integration with your own data. Available from a minimum three-month term at 300,000 yen per month.

Program 02 | Run the whole department on AI
Embed AI into your operating workflows and make it stick across an entire department over six months. Built for executives, DX leads, and department heads at mid-sized companies, it covers department-level AI rollout, structuring of internal data, and redesign of operating workflows. Available from a six-month term at 500,000 yen per month.

Program 03 | An adoption blueprint to make the whole company AI-driven
Roll out AI department by department and build a company-wide, AI-driven operating model. Built for executive leadership and DX division heads at listed and large enterprises, it provides end-to-end support — from drafting a department-by-department adoption plan through phased implementation and connection to management KPIs. We support the transition from PoC to full engagement; pricing is by consultation.

What every program has in common

Every program is built on the approach we call context engineering. By structuring your internal operational knowledge, decision criteria, and unwritten rules into a form AI can understand and act on, we deliver real, working results that tool adoption alone cannot.

We also support all major AI tools, including Claude, ChatGPT, and Microsoft Copilot, and advise from a neutral position that isn't tied to any single tool.

For full details, see the Services page on our website, or reach out anytime through our contact form.

The right program depends on where your company stands.

Pricing, timeline, and approach all start with a free consultation. We'll hear out your current challenges and map the best way to begin, on the spot.
