CEO Kotaro Furuno delivered a keynote seminar at Sakura Internet Inc.

Kotaro Furuno, CEO of TechWorker Inc. — a company providing generative AI adoption support — served as the lead speaker at an internal seminar on generative AI held by Sakura Internet Inc.

Seminar Overview

Held for the employees of Sakura Internet Inc., the seminar was designed to build foundational knowledge of generative AI and encourage its use in day-to-day work. TechWorker was invited as an external speaker on the strength of its experience driving generative AI projects at multiple listed companies.

The seminar covered a wide range of ground, from how generative AI fundamentally works to concrete ways to apply it in practice. We structured the session so that participants could try the AI tools hands-on and form a clear picture of how to apply them to their own work.

Topics Covered

  • Foundations of generative AI and the latest developments
  • Concrete examples of generative AI applied to real work
  • An approach to "organizing context" — connecting internal data to AI
  • Key precautions for using AI tools safely at work
  • A hands-on, practical workshop

What's Next

At the next seminar, we plan to share enterprise use cases for generative AI along with the legal considerations involved. We are also planning a contribution to "Saku-Nare," the owned media run by Sakura Internet Inc.

A Word from CEO Furuno

"Generative AI has the potential to transform every industry. In this seminar, I wanted to convey above all the importance of simply trying it and getting hands-on. AI capabilities improve by the day, and how quickly a company can build this technology into its operations will, I believe, determine its competitiveness going forward."

Under the banner "AI is the engine. Context is the fuel," TechWorker Inc. has supported the generative AI initiatives of 37 companies and 2,500 people, including listed enterprises. We also welcome inquiries for corporate training and seminar engagements.
