Free webinar: "The Pitfalls of AI Adoption"

TechWorker Inc. is hosting a free webinar titled "The Pitfalls of AI Adoption -- Why Deploying Tools Alone Does Not Deliver Results" for executives, DX leaders, and IT department heads.

Event Details

TitleThe Pitfalls of AI Adoption -- Why Deploying Tools Alone Does Not Deliver Results
DateMarch 28, 2026 (Sat) 14:00 - 15:30 JST
FormatOnline (Zoom)
FeeFree
Capacity100 seats (first-come, first-served)
AudienceExecutives, DX leaders, IT department heads
SpeakerKotaro Furuno (CEO, TechWorker Inc.)

What You Will Learn

Companies that deploy generative AI tools but fail to see results share common patterns. In this webinar, we will present the five most frequent failure patterns identified across our 37-company portfolio, along with practical solutions for each.

  • Pattern 1: Over-investing in tool selection while under-investing in post-deployment design
  • Pattern 2: Usage concentrated among a few AI-savvy employees, with no organization-wide adoption
  • Pattern 3: Testing with generic prompts that fail to address company-specific workflows
  • Pattern 4: Completing a PoC but never integrating AI into production workflows
  • Pattern 5: Vague success metrics that make it impossible to demonstrate ROI to leadership

Who Should Attend

  • Organizations that deployed ChatGPT or Copilot but see low utilization rates
  • DX leaders planning company-wide AI rollout but uncertain about the approach
  • Teams that completed a PoC but lack a clear path to the next stage
  • Executives who need to justify AI investment outcomes to the board

How to Register

To register for the webinar, please submit your details through our contact form. A Zoom link will be sent to registered participants by the day before the event.
